About Shatranj' 2.19

Shatranj' 2.19 is the freshers welcome event of Electrical Engineering Department of B.P.PODDAR INSTITUTE OF MANAGEMENT AND TECHNOLOGY, Kolkata.

Over the years, Freshers welcome by Electrical Department has established a legacy of fellowship and pride which fosters cultural diversity and stimulates creativity and imagination.

This spring we unveil a event which shall witness days of absolute ecstasy, providing the budding artists a competing platform in diverse fields such as music, dance, theatre, photography, literature.

So get ready to bask in the glory of a magnificence of an unprecedented scale and embrace all the fun and frolic because ladies and gentlemen, Shatranj' 2.19 will be unparalleled! Let make this event memorable.
